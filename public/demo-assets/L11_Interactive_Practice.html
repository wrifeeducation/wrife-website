W2 Activity 6: Subject or Object?

Quick Tip!

The SUBJECT does the action. The OBJECT receives the action.

In this sentence, is "the tractor" the subject or the object?
Mr. Brown showed us his tractor.
Subject (it does the action)
Object (it receives the action)
